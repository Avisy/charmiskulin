Dukie

Dukie is a 4 year old Morkie, half Maltese, and half Yorkie. He is owned and loved by my daughter Cally. She brings him over to visit, and he is fascinated by the fish in the pond. He likes to pose for pictures and is very easy for me to paint. 16’x20’ canvas in oil.
