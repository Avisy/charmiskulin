I have always been the most comfortable in the company of animals and painting them comes easy to me.

I love painting all animals, but horses and dogs are my favorite.

I won the People's choce award at the 2015 Rio Brazos Juried Art Show in Granbury, TX with 'He Doesn't Know it's Me' ( a horse painting )

I live on a small ranch just north of Fort Worth. My two little mares, one German Shepherd, an orange barn cat, and a pond full of fish are my constant companions.

Lonely Cow Dark

In “Lonely Cow Dark”, the trailer in the background will likely be used to take him to processing. Lonely cow is actually a calf that was weaned from his mother near Boyd, Texas. He would see me drive up in my little black car and was convinced it was his mom. He would run to the fence and call out to my car. This little calf was alone in a massive pasture and wanted to make a connection with someone.

24”x48” canvas in oil (2014)
