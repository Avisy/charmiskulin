Char Miskulin

He Doesn't Know it's Me

When I was a little girl, I loved horses with all my heart. Being raised outside of Chicago, there were no real horses within a 50 mile radius. To this day, I still have a love for horses and they are my all time favorite subject to paint. The subject is Beau my very first horse. I painted in the moment before he recognises me, someday, in heaven.

36”x48” canvas in oil (2015)
