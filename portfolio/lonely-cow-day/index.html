Lonely Cow Day

In “Lonely Cow Day”, the barn in the distance offers shelter without companionship. Lonely cow is actually a calf that was weaned from his mother near Boyd, Texas. He would see me drive up in my little black car and was convinced it was his mom. He would run to the fence and call out to my car. This little calf was alone in a massive pasture and wanted to make a connection with someone.

24”x48” canvas in oil (2014)
