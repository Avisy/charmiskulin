Char Miskulin

Fred 1

Fred was a .17 cent feeder goldfish I bought to put in the horse water trough. It was getting close to winter, so I brought him inside and plopped him into an aquarium. He was the most social fish I have ever met. He would actually look at you for long periods of time. Spring came and Fred was placed into his own pond. He has a new best friend and companion, a .24 cent feeder goldfish, Amanda.

24”x48” Oil on canvas, 2014
